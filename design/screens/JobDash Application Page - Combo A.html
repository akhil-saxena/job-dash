About the role

As an Experienced Software Engineer at Automattic, you'll join one of our many distributed teams — Divisions — working on the products that power roughly 40% of the web: WordPress.com, Jetpack, WooCommerce, Tumblr, and more.

We value written communication over synchronous meetings. Most of your day-to-day is coding, code review, and asynchronous discussion on internal P2s.

What you'll work on

Divisions are small, cross-functional, and autonomous. You pick the one that fits. Examples: WP.com Performance, Jetpack Security, Woo Payments, Simple Note, Day One. Rotation is encouraged.

Requirements

React · 5y TypeScript · 4y PHP · 3y REST APIs Async comm WordPress ecosystem GraphQL at scale
My fit notes

Strong match on frontend + TypeScript. Gap on WordPress internals — worth spending a weekend on the WP Gutenberg codebase before the tech screen. The async-first culture aligns perfectly with my remote-only preference.
