A1 — Basic Classic Sidebar

Clean and simple. Logo, nav items, header with actions. No extra information in the sidebar — just navigation. This is the starting point.

  • 7 nav items, no counts or badges
  • Fixed sidebar, no collapse
  • Search only in header or Cmd+K
  • No pipeline summary visible
  • No quick access to pinned apps
cleansimplefewer distractions

A2 — Enhanced Classic Sidebar

Same classic layout but the sidebar earns its screen space. Every pixel adds awareness without adding clutter. 6 improvements over basic:

  • Search + Cmd+K always visible in sidebar — find anything instantly
  • Pinned apps section — your 3-5 focus applications one click away
  • Contextual counts on nav items — Calendar: 3, Kanban: 24
  • Pipeline mini-bar in footer — see status distribution at a glance
  • Collapse toggle — shrink to icon-only when you need content space
  • Today's date in header — temporal context for deadline awareness
